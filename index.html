About

Bagpipe:
A wind instrument consisting of a reed melody pipe and from one to five drones with air supplied continuously either by a bag with valve-stopped mouth tube or by bellows

Our music is defined as a primarily irish resist music utilizing traditional, acoustic instruments. Commonly the topics involve the plight of the common people, including depression and oppression.

Irish worker pride!
